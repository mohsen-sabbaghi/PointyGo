Problem And Solution

Problem Description:

We believe that Not only the covid-19 pandemic but also nowadays with technologies progress people tend to inactivity that has made them lazy, so it can cause mental and physical diseases. The main purpose of my product is to encourage and motivate people to do physical activities. so, it can be bringing people together, and also, it can help society to improve their mental and physical health.
